5. AI Coach: Nova and Third-Party AI Processing

Nova is NovaFit's AI-powered fitness coach. She is powered by OpenAI's GPT language models. When you interact with Nova, your data is sent to OpenAI, a third-party AI service, for processing. This section explains exactly what data is shared, how it is used, and your rights regarding this data sharing.

Important: By using Nova, your personal data including chat messages, meal photos, and fitness profile information is sent to OpenAI (a third-party AI service) for processing. You will be asked to provide explicit consent before your first interaction with Nova. You can use all other features of NovaFit without using Nova and without any data being sent to OpenAI.

5.1 Who Receives Your Data

Your Nova chat data is sent to OpenAI, L.L.C. (headquartered in San Francisco, California, USA) through their API. The data is routed through our secure Cloudflare Worker proxy, which acts as an intermediary between the app and OpenAI. At no point is your data sent to any other AI provider or third party for processing.

5.2 What Data Is Sent to OpenAI

When you send a message to Nova, the following information is included in the request sent to OpenAI's API:

  • Your chat message text
  • Any meal photos you share with Nova (images are sent for calorie and nutrition analysis)
  • Your display name
  • Your current streak count
  • Today's workout plan and recent workout history
  • Today's nutrition totals and logged meals
  • Your height, weight, and training setup
  • Your focus muscles and training schedule

This context is necessary for Nova to provide personalized, relevant coaching responses rather than generic advice.

5.3 How OpenAI Uses Your Data

OpenAI processes the data we send solely to generate a response to your message. According to OpenAI's API data usage policy (as of the date of this policy):

  • OpenAI does not use data submitted through their API to train or improve their models
  • API data is retained by OpenAI for up to 30 days for abuse and misuse monitoring, after which it is deleted
  • OpenAI does not share your data with other third parties

We encourage you to review OpenAI's API Data Usage Policies and OpenAI's Privacy Policy for the most current information.

5.4 Data We Do NOT Send to OpenAI

  • Your email address or Apple ID
  • Your device identifier or IP address
  • Your payment or purchase information
  • Your location data
  • Any data from other apps on your device

5.5 Nova's Limitations

Nova is an AI assistant and her responses may not always be accurate, medically sound, or appropriate for your specific health condition. See Section 7 for important health and fitness disclaimers.

Nova is AI-powered and can make mistakes. Use her guidance as a starting point, not a substitute for professional advice.

5.6 Harmful Content Filters

We have implemented filters to prevent Nova from responding to messages related to self-harm, eating disorders, or other harmful topics. If such content is detected, Nova will respond with a safe, supportive message and encourage you to seek professional help.

If you choose not to consent to AI data sharing, you can still use every other feature of NovaFit. Only the Nova chat feature requires this consent. You will not be penalized or restricted from using non-AI features.

8. Data Storage and Security

8.1 Where Your Data Is Stored

Your personal data, including your profile, workout history, streak data, nutrition logs, and achievements, is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database service. Supabase stores data on servers managed by Amazon Web Services (AWS). Your data is protected by authentication and access controls so that only you can access your own data.

8.2 Data In Transit

All data transmitted between the App and our services (Supabase, OpenAI via Cloudflare) is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS encryption. Your data is never transmitted in plain text.

8.3 Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information, including encrypted data transmission, authenticated API access, and row-level security policies on our database. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We recommend keeping your device protected with a passcode or biometric lock.

8.4 Data Retention

Your data is retained in our systems for as long as you maintain an active account. If you delete your account or request data deletion, your data will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days. OpenAI retains API data for up to 30 days for abuse monitoring before deletion.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to any third parties. We do not share your data with advertisers. The following third-party services receive data as part of the App's functionality:
