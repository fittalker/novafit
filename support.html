Frequently Asked Questions

What is Nova, the AI coach?

Nova is your personal AI fitness coach inside NovaFit. It provides workout recommendations, nutrition guidance, and motivation throughout your 180-day transformation. All of Nova's advice is based on established guidelines from organizations like ACSM, NSCA, and WHO.

How does the 180-day program work?

The program is structured into challenge-gated phases. You progress by completing workouts, hitting calorie targets, and maintaining streaks. Each phase unlocks new routines and challenges tailored to your fitness level.

How are my calories and workouts calculated?

NovaFit uses the Mifflin-St Jeor equation to estimate your daily maintenance calories based on your age, weight, height, and activity level. Workout plans are built using evidence-based guidelines from ACSM and NSCA. You can find detailed sources on our Sources & Citations page.

Is my data safe?

Yes. Your personal data stays on your device and our secure servers. We do not sell or share your information with third parties. Review our full Privacy Policy for details.

How do I cancel or manage my purchase?

NovaFit uses a one-time purchase model through the App Store. If you need a refund, you can request one through Apple's standard refund process in your purchase history.

The app isn't working properly. What should I do?

Try closing and reopening the app, or restarting your device. If the issue persists, email us at masfitsolutions@gmail.com with a description of the problem and your device model. Screenshots help us diagnose faster.

Can I use NovaFit if I have a medical condition?

NovaFit provides general fitness guidance and is not a substitute for professional medical advice. If you have a medical condition or injury, consult your healthcare provider before starting any fitness program.
